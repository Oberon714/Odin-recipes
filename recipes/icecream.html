Pizza Time

Description

Ice cream is a frozen dessert typically made from milk or cream that has been flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice, such as cocoa or vanilla, or with fruit, such as strawberries or peaches..

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream (or regular cream)
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2-3/4 cups sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Step 1: Whisk all ingredients together and process according to your ice cream machines directions.
    2. Step 2: If desired, add toppings or mix-ins during the last minute of churning.
    3. Step 3: Freeze the mixture in the freezer until firm.
