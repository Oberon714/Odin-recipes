Pizza Time

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour is a fundamental ingredient in pizza dough.
  • Yeast: Active dry yeast or instant yeast is used to leaven the dough.
  • Olive Oil: Used for flavor and to keep the crust soft.
  • Salt: Used to enhance flavor and texture.
  • Sugar: Some recipes include a small amount of sugar to help fermentation.
  • Tomatoes: Fresh or canned, tomatoes are a primary topping.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella, cheddar, Parmesan, and other types are commonly used.
  • Onion: Fresh or caramelized, onions add flavor and texture.
  • Garlic: Minced or crushed, garlic is a popular flavor component.
  • Herbs: Fresh or dried, herbs like oregano, basil, and rosemary are used for flavor.
  • Meat: Pepperoni, sausage, bacon, and beef mince are common protein toppings.
  • Vegetables: Mushrooms, capsicum, olives, and aubergines are popular vegetable toppings.
  • Pesto: A sauce made from basil, garlic, and olive oil, often used as a topping.
  • Ricotta: Used as a topping or mixed with other ingredients for a creamy sauce.
